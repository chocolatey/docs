## External Login Settings

1. Open the Chocolatey Central Management Site in the browser.
1. Login with the `ccmadmin` user.
1. In the left sidebar menu click on **Administration**and then **Settings**.
1. Click on the **User Management** tab in the **Settings** screen.
1. Under External Login Settings click the **Enable social logins** checkbox.
1. From the list of available external login providers (currently Facebook, Google, X, and Microsoft) click the checkbox for each one that should be used.
1. Enter all necessary information (use the **Show** button to confirm secret entries)

    {/* The URL of this image HAS to change. This is used just to get the page to render */}
    ![Enable External Login Settings](https://img.chocolatey.org/docs/ccm-playwright/administration/settings/user-management-modal-ldap-password.png)

1. Click the **Save all** button at the bottom left of the page to save your settings.

After logging out of the Chocolatey Central Management Site, new options will be shown to allow logging in with the selected External Login providers.

{/* The URL of this image HAS to change. This is used just to get the page to render */}
![Login screen showing options to login with selected External Login providers](https://img.chocolatey.org/docs/ccm-playwright/administration/settings/user-management-modal-ldap-password.png)

## LDAP Configuration

<Callout type="info">
    The Central Management Server must be joined to the Active Directory Domain.
</Callout>

1. Open the Chocolatey Central Management Site in the browser.
1. Login with the `ccmadmin` user.
1. In the left sidebar menu click on **Administration**and then **Settings**.
1. Click on the **User Management** tab in the **Settings** screen.
1. Under LDAP Setting click the **Enable LDAP authentication** checkbox.
1. If LDAPS (rather than bare LDAP) is to be used, ensure to check the **Use SSL** checkbox. Fill in your FQDN for the `Domain name` field and the `User name` field with an active directory account that has access to query user accounts within your active directory environment.

    ![Chocolatey Central Management LDAP Setup](https://img.chocolatey.org/docs/ccm-playwright/administration/settings/user-management-checkbox-enable-ldap.png)

1. Click the **Update LDAP password** button to open a modal window to allow you to enter/confirm the password that is to be used.

    ![Update LDAP Password](https://img.chocolatey.org/docs/ccm-playwright/administration/settings/user-management-modal-ldap-password.png)

1. Click the **Save** button
1. Click the **Save all** button at the bottom left of the page to save your settings.

<Callout type="info">
    In order for LDAP authentication to succeed in versions of Central Management 0.3.1 and lower
    an Email Address, Surname, and GivenName must be configured on the properties of the Active Directory user you are
    attempting to use for login. If any of these fields are empty, errors will be encountered when attempting to login
    to the Central Management application.
</Callout>

## Passwordless Login

1. Open the Chocolatey Central Management Site in the browser.
1. Login with the `ccmadmin` user.
1. In the left sidebar menu click on **Administration**and then **Settings**.
1. Click on the **User Management** tab in the **Settings** screen.
1. Under Passwordless Login click the **Enable passwordless login via email** checkbox.

    {/* The URL of this image HAS to change. This is used just to get the page to render */}
    ![Enable Passwordless Login checkbox activated](https://img.chocolatey.org/docs/ccm-playwright/administration/settings/user-management-modal-ldap-password.png)

1. Click the **Save all** button at the bottom left of the page to save your settings.

After logging out of the Chocolatey Central Management Site, a new option will be shown to allow logging in without using a password.

{/* The URL of this image HAS to change. This is used just to get the page to render */}
![Login screen showing option to login without using a password](https://img.chocolatey.org/docs/ccm-playwright/administration/settings/user-management-modal-ldap-password.png)

Clicking the **Continue without a password** button directs the user to a new page where they can enter the email to send a confirmation code to. If this email address is matched to a user on the site, a confirmation code will be sent to the email address.

{/* The URL of this image HAS to change. This is used just to get the page to render */}
![Passwordless Login page](https://img.chocolatey.org/docs/ccm-playwright/administration/settings/user-management-modal-ldap-password.png)

The user will have a 5 minute window where they will be able to enter the verification code.

{/* The URL of this image HAS to change. This is used just to get the page to render */}
![Verify Passwordless Code page](https://img.chocolatey.org/docs/ccm-playwright/administration/settings/user-management-modal-ldap-password.png)
